*(What is the total addressable wallet for engagement in our footprint?)*

- **F&B Venue Wallet ($2.88B):** Based on the $300/mo acquisition/retention spend of 800K strictly organized venues (ignoring the 400K semi-organized venues entirely).
- **Multi-Vertical Wallet ($1.72B):** Segmented wallet capture across Retail ($150/mo), Wellness ($100/mo), and Coworking/Lounges ($200/mo).

- **Consumer Transactions ($160M):** 15% GMV capture for payments (1.5% MDR) and realistic table booking volumes across the F&B dataset.
- **Web3 Enterprise Infra ($30.5M):** Node hosting and L2 transaction fees for enterprises tokenizing their own loyalty ecosystems via CheckIn's architecture + swap fees.

**Total Verified Revenue TAM:** **$4.898 Billion ($4.9B)**

*Non-Revenue GNV Disclaimer:* The $2.5B APAC Tokenized Loyalty Market represents the total target **Gross Network Value (GNV)** of the Global Credits liquidity pool. This dictates platform valuation multiples, but is restricted from the Revenue TAM calculation.

### 2. Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM): The Realistic Target
*(What portion of the TAM can we capture with our current product and GTM strategy in the next 3-5 years?)*

- **F&B Sub-SAM ($339M):** Strictly filtered to 211,200 venues across the Top 20 Metros that meet two criteria: Avg Bill Value >₹500 and Modern POS adoption. 
- **Multi-Vertical Sub-SAM ($70M):** Limited purely to lifestyle adjacencies sharing the exact F&B consumer base (Premium Gyms, Bars, Coworking) in metros. Retail excluded.
- **Brand/Consumer Sub-SAM ($80M):** Targeting only the absolute top 10,000 premium venues for FMCG quests and early payment adoption.

**Total Verified Revenue SAM:** **$489 Million** (Roughly 10% of TAM)
