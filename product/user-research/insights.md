# Research Insights — Synthesized

> Last updated: Feb 16, 2026

## Key Market Insights

### Consumer Behavior
- 74% of Gen-Z value real-world experiences over digital ones
- 70% of Gen-Z identify TikTok as top platform for food recommendations
- Gen-Z trusts friend recommendations 50% more than influencers
- Users engage with only 10–15 apps daily; 96% uninstall apps within first month
- 90% of Millennials/Gen-Z require brand authenticity

### Loyalty Industry
- 41% of consumers cite loyalty programs as primary reason for staying loyal
- 81% of shoppers have abandoned brands in the past year
- Over 50% of loyalty memberships become inactive
- Gamification success: Chipotle achieved $2B+ in digital sales through challenges; BrewDog saw 400% purchase frequency increase through badges

### Venue Economics
- Aggregator commissions: India avg 27.8% (traditional), up to 37% (cloud kitchens)
- Customer acquisition costs up 60% over 5 years (avg $606)
- Restaurant retention at 55% vs. 75–84% in other sectors
- New customer acquisition 5–7x more expensive than retention
- Restaurant marketing budget: 3–6% of revenue (6–10% for new establishments)

### Social Commerce
- Social commerce projected to exceed $1 trillion by 2028 (from $570B in 2023)
- FLUFF achieved 89% conversion increases and 98% AOV improvements through phygital
- 30% sales conversion increase from well-executed phygital strategies
