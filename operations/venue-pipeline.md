## Onboarding Process

1. RM-led high-touch introduction and demo
2. Dashboard setup (venue profile, branding, initial quests)
3. Staff training (payment/validation flows, analytics)
4. QR code and table materials deployment
5. Launch day co-marketing support
6. Week 1 check-in and optimization
7. Monthly review and quest refresh

## Target Milestones

| Period | Target | Strategy |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Pre-raise (Now → Apr 2026) | 5–10 verbal commitments from interviews | Customer discovery doubles as pipeline building |
| M0–6 (Post-raise) | 10–15 initial commitments, 5–10 active | RM hire + founder-led sales |
| M6–12 | 50–75 active venues | RM scaling, freemium self-service starting |
| M12–18 | 150–200 active venues | Product-led growth, self-service onboarding |
